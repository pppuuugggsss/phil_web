When is the best time to go?

The best season to visit the Philippines is during the dry season, which typically runs from November to April. This period offers pleasant weather, with less humidity and minimal rainfall, making it ideal for exploring the countrys stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes. During these months, travelers can enjoy activities such as island hopping in Palawan, diving in Cebu, and soaking up the sun in Boracay without the interruptions of heavy rain. Additionally, the dry season coincides with many local festivals, providing visitors with a unique opportunity to experience Philippine traditions and hospitality at their finest.

I remembered how beautiful this island was when I visited with my parents a few years ago, and I knew I wanted to share that experience with Martin. So, from July 6th to July 11th, we have decided that Coron is the place for us!

Siquijor

This island has always had a mysterious reputation... stories of magic, healers, and witches have made many people wary of it. But that only made the idea of visiting even more exciting for me. Unlike the more touristy spots we considered, this place promised a real adventure. So, from July 12th to July 15th, we felt it was the perfect time to explore it!

Siargao

How could we possibly skip this island, especially when we both love reggae and Martin's passionate about surfing? As the saying goes, save the best for last! From July 16th to July 25th, we wanted to fully embrace the island lifestyle... slow mornings, salty hair, and sunsets that feel like forever. It is the perfect way to end our adventure on a high note.
